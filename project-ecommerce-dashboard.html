E-Commerce Sales Dashboard

Level: BI Dashboard • Tools: Tableau Public • Role: Dashboard designer & analyst

Overview

This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing online retail transaction data from a UK-based e-commerce company. The dashboard enables users to explore sales trends, geographic distribution, and product performance through dynamic filtering and visualization.

The objective is to provide at-a-glance KPIs alongside drill-down capabilities for deeper business insights.

Live Dashboard

View Interactive Dashboard on Tableau Public →

Dataset

The dataset contains transactional records from a UK-based online retailer, spanning December 2010 to December 2011. Each record includes invoice details, product information, quantity, pricing, and customer location.

  • Source: Kaggle — E-Commerce Data
  • Records: ~540,000 transactions
  • Fields: InvoiceNo, StockCode, Description, Quantity, InvoiceDate, UnitPrice, CustomerID, Country

Dashboard Features

  • KPI Cards: Total Revenue, Order Count, Customer Count
  • Sales Trend: Monthly revenue over time (line chart)
  • Revenue by Country: Geographic distribution (map)
  • Top Products: Top 10 products by revenue (bar chart)
  • Interactivity: Click-to-filter functionality across all charts

Key Insights

  • The United Kingdom dominates revenue, accounting for the vast majority of sales.
  • Revenue shows seasonal patterns with notable peaks in late 2011.
  • A small number of products drive a significant portion of total revenue.
  • The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly identify regional and product trends.

Skills Demonstrated

  • Connecting and preparing data in Tableau
  • Creating calculated fields (Revenue = Quantity × Unit Price)
  • Building multiple visualization types (KPIs, line charts, maps, bar charts)
  • Designing effective dashboard layouts
  • Implementing interactive filters for user exploration
